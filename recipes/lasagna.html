Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese. This hearty and delicious meal is perfect for family dinners and gatherings.

Our lasagna recipe is easy to follow and results in a mouthwatering dish that everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef and garlic until browned. Drain the excess fat.
  4. Add the marinara sauce to the skillet and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, egg, salt, and pepper.
  6. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
  7. Layer with noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella cheese, and meat sauce. Repeat layers.
  8. Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  9. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  10. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  11. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving. Garnish with chopped parsley.
